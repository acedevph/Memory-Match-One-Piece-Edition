## ğŸ¯ How to Play

1. Click on any difficulty level button (Easy, Normal, or Hard)
2. The game board will appear with cards face down
3. Click on a card to flip it and reveal the image
4. Click on another card to find its match
5. If the cards match, they stay flipped
6. If they don't match, they flip back after 1 second
7. Continue until all pairs are matched
8. Try to complete the game in the least moves and fastest time!
